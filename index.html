Dare Generator


Game rules; Optional

1. The oldest player goes first.
2. Each dare you choose is worth points.
(Easy: 1, Medium: 2, Hard: 3 and Very Hard: 5)
3. Each player starts with three chickens but can earn more throughout the game.
4. Every time you use a chicken to avoid a dare you lose it.
5. Once a player runs out of chickens, they are out of the game and end with their current point total
6. Whoever ends with the highest point total wins!
